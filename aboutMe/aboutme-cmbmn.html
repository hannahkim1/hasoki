여름

여름 (pronounced yeo-rum) is my Korean name. 여름 means summer in Korean.

my birth certificate technically says my name is "hannah sofia kim", without the 여름. that is because my parents did not give me this name when I was born.

I was given this name by myself, my parents, and my friends. I grew up speaking only English, so I unfortunately, I wasn't involved with Korean culture and language in my early years.

It wasn't until I was 14 that I became more in touch with my ethnic background. I gave myself the name 여름 because I truly thrive in the summer months. My friends suggested this name for me, and my parents approved.

i am proud of my names and i love how all of my names are relatively common, but uniquely me.
